🎮 Game Title: “Shadows of the Thousand Doors”

🧭 Genre:

Story-driven Text RPG
Adventure • Mystery • Survival
Replayable multiple-path game

⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻

📜 Story

The city of “Thousand Doors” is not recorded on any map. Every night, one of its doors opens and releases a new reality—sometimes peaceful worlds, sometimes living nightmares.

You are a Door Traveler, someone who can see hidden doors. But there is a price: every time you pass through a door, you lose part of your memory.

Now you stand at the center of the city. Three doors appear before you:

* A blue metallic door (cold, technological)
* A broken wooden door (ancient, whispering)
* A pure white door (no handle, unknown)

A voice echoes in your mind:

“One door saves you… one shapes you… one erases you forever.”

⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻⸻

📊 Game System

You have 3 stats:

* ❤️ Health: 100
* 🧠 Memory: 100
* ⚡ Energy: 100

Every choice affects these stats.
If any stat reaches 0 → Game Over.

⸻

🎯 START OF THE GAME

Cold wind moves through the empty city square. The three doors tremble slightly as if alive.

⸻

🚪 YOUR FIRST CHOICE:

Which door do you choose?

1) 🔵 Blue Metal Door

2) 🪵 Broken Wooden Door

3) ⚪ White Unknown Door

Just reply with the number.

⸻

🔵 If you choose the Blue Metal Door…

You enter an underground laboratory filled with neon lights and endless machines.

An Artificial Intelligence speaks:

“You volunteered for the Memory Experiment. Do you want to know the truth of this city?”

Choices:

* 1.	Yes, show me the truth
* 2.	Run away
* 3.	Hack the system

Outcomes:

* Truth → High knowledge gain, memory loss
* Escape → Health increase, energy loss
* Hack → High risk, high reward

⸻

🪵 If you choose the Broken Wooden Door…

You step into a dark forest where the trees seem to breathe.

A blind old man appears:

“You are late… in this forest, memories are hunted.”

He offers you a sword.

Choices:

* 1.	Take the sword and continue
* 2.	Refuse the sword
* 3.	Attack the old man

Outcomes:

* Sword → Strength increases, memory decreases
* Refuse → Memory increases, danger increases
* Attack → High risk: death or major reward

⸻

⚪ If you choose the White Unknown Door…

There is nothing.

No ground. No sky. Only pure white space.

A voice says:

“This is not the end of the game… it is the beginning of rewriting it.”

You can now reshape reality.

Choices:

* 1.	Create a safe world
* 2.	Create a dangerous but powerful world
* 3.	Exit the game

Outcomes:

* Safe world → High survival, low challenge
* Dangerous world → High power, fast danger
* Exit → Game ends

⸻

🧠 Hidden Mechanics

If your Memory reaches 0:
You lose your identity and become a new “door” in the city.

If your Health reaches 0:
Your body is trapped inside one of the worlds forever.

If your Energy reaches 0:
You fall into an eternal sleep in a random dimension.

⸻

🎯 MAIN OBJECTIVE (SECRET)

Your real goal is not just choosing doors…

You must find the “True Door of the Thousand City”.

It only appears when:

* You fail or “die” at least twice
* You discover at least one major truth
* You break one door completely
